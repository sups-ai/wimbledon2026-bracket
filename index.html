How it works

Fill out the full draw for both ATP and WTA — pick a winner in every match from Round 1 through the Final. Your winners carry forward automatically, so a round unlocks once the round before it is complete.

Quarterfinal redraw & late entry

Picks lock Monday June 29 at 6 AM ET, when first-round play begins. Once the Round of 16 is complete, a quarterfinal window opens — Monday July 6 in the evening, closing Tuesday July 7 at 6 AM ET, before quarterfinal play. During that window you can redraw: re-pick the quarterfinals through the final for 100 points, keeping everything you scored through the Round of 16. New players can also join late for the quarterfinals onward, starting at −100 points and completing all remaining picks to be scored. Each draw (ATP and WTA) is separate, and if you do nothing your original bracket stands. Redrawn brackets and late entries are marked on the leaderboard.

Tiebreaker

If two entries finish level on points, the first tiebreak is the closest guess to the number of games in the final set of the championship match (possible values: 6, 7, 8, 9, 10, 12, or 13). If still level, it goes to the total number of matches picked correctly. Standings are never broken by when you entered.

Results update automatically. Once the draw is published and matches begin, winners and scores refresh on their own throughout the fortnight.
